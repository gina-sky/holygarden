**Endangerment Attacks  
  
危害攻击**

**10\. Drasticizing/Catastrophizing/Hypochondriasizing**. _I feel afraid but I am not in danger. I am not “in trouble” with my parents. [[null|]]I will not blow things out of proportion. I refuse to scare myself with thoughts and pictures of my life deteriorating. No more home-made horror movies and disaster flicks. I will not turn every ache and pain into a story about my imminent demise. I am safe and at peace._  
  
10\. 极端化/灾难化/疑病化。我感到害怕，但我并不处于危险之中。我和父母并没有麻烦。我不会夸大事态。我拒绝用我生活恶化的想法和画面吓唬自己。不再有自制的恐怖电影和灾难片。我不会把每一个疼痛和不适都变成关于我即将死亡的故事。我安全且平静。

**11\. Negative focus**_. I renounce over-noticing and dwelling on what might be wrong with me or life around me. I will not minimize or discount my attributes. Right now, I notice, visualize and enumerate my accomplishments, talents and qualities, as well as the many gifts that life offers me, e.g., nature, music, film, food, beauty, color, friends, pets, etc._  
  
11\. 负面焦点。我放弃过度关注和沉浸在我可能存在的问题或周围生活中的问题。我不会贬低或低估我的特质。现在，我注意到、视觉化和列举我的成就、才能和品质，以及生活给予我的许多礼物，例如：自然、音乐、电影、食物、美丽、色彩、朋友、宠物等。

**12\. Time Urgency**_. I am not in danger. I do not need to rush. I will not hurry unless it is a true emergency. I am learning to enjoy doing my daily activities at a relaxed pace._  
  
12\. 时间紧迫感。我没有危险。我不需要匆忙。除非是真正的紧急情况，否则我不会急忙行事。我正在学习以轻松的节奏享受我的日常活动。

**13\. Disabling Performance Anxiety**_. I reduce procrastination by reminding myself that I will not accept unfair criticism or perfectionist expectations from anyone. Even when afraid, I will defend myself from unfair criticism. I won’t let fear make my decisions._  
  
13\. 消除表现焦虑。我通过提醒自己，我不会接受任何人的不公平批评或完美主义期望来减少拖延。即使害怕，我也会为自己辩护，不受不公平批评的影响。我不会让恐惧左右我的决定。

**14\. Perseverating About Being Attacked**. _Unless there are clear signs of danger, I will thought-stop my projection of past bullies/critics onto others. The vast majority of my fellow human beings are peaceful people. I have legal authorities to aid in my protection if threatened by the few who aren’t. I invoke thoughts and images of my friends’ love and support._  
  
14\. 不断思考被攻击。除非有明显的危险迹象，否则我会停止将过去的欺凌者/批评者投射到他人身上。我大多数同胞都是和平的人。如果受到少数不和平的人的威胁，我有法律当局来帮助我保护自己。我召唤起朋友们的爱和支

Critic attacks like most things are not all-or-none. They can vary in intensity and duration. Most of the case examples that follow are on the intense end of the spectrum. I chose them because they are more illustrative of how the critic operates. Once you become proficient at identifying intense critic attacks, you typically develop the mindfulness necessary to notice more subtle attacks. This is essential because most survivors spend tremendous amounts of time barely conscious of how incessantly self-critical they are.  
  
批评攻击就像大多数事情一样，并非全有或全无。它们的强度和持续时间可以有所不同。以下的大多数案例都是处于强烈的一端。我选择它们是因为它们更能说明批评者是如何运作的。一旦你擅长识别强烈的批评攻击，通常就会发展出必要的正念，以注意到更微妙的攻击。这是至关重要的，因为大多数幸存者花费大量时间几乎意识不到自己无休止的自我批评。

[[null|]]As with the flashback management steps, memorizing these rebuttals to the critic and using them like mantras is especially helpful in critic shrinking.
