**Reparenting  
  
重新父化**

Reparenting is a key aspect of relational healing. It is primarily a process of addressing the many developmentally arrested needs of the traumatized child that we were. In this book we repeatedly address the two most fundamental of these needs: love and protection.  
  
重归父母是关系疗愈的关键方面。它主要是一个解决我们作为创伤儿童所存在的许多发展停滞需求的过程。在这本书中，我们反复探讨这些需求中最基本的两个：爱和保护。

“Suggested Intentions for Recovery” \[[Toolbox 1. Suggested Intentions For Recovery|Toolbox 1]] in chapter 16\] renders yet another picture of the diverse developmental arrests that may as yet be unaddressed in the Cptsd survivor. The toolbox presents these needs as tangible goals that we can use to direct our recovery efforts.  
  
“康复建议意图” \[第 16 章工具箱 1\] 展现了 Cptsd 幸存者可能尚未解决的多种发展停滞情况。工具箱将这些需求呈现为我们可以用来指导我们康复努力的切实目标。
